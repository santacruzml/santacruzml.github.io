Group Information

Santa Cruz Machine Learning is an organization dedicated to fostering students interested in machine learning, data science, and artificial intelligence by providing resources, hosting speakers, and connecting students with faculty. We also facilitate collaboration between researchers in need of machine learning and practitioners. To be added to our mailing list and slack channel, please click sign up above and fill out our entry form.

Got Data?

Work with us to analyze your dataset.
